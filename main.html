About Me

In my free time, I am super passionate about travel and seeing the world. I have been to over 20 countries and counting, and I plan on studying abroad over the next year or so! I love to play basketball and volleyball, am an avid NBA and MLB enjoyer, and love photographic and videographic arts. I pride myself with my ability to combine creativity and reasoning in many aspects of my life. I believe these traits are some of those that truly defines a strong computer scientist.
